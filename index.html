    Agro Electronic Devices And AI (AEDAA equipments) is pvt Ltd company, managed Thota durgaprasad,Thota murali originated from Gattu village in Madanapalle, Andhra Pradesh.
    And incubated at AIC PECF,pondicherry Technological university and N.G. Ranga University(RKVY Rafter).
    We are building next-generation Farming, through technology and AI.
    We Believe in getting full potential of the crop , than gene modification.
    We are specialized in Crop specific Water optimization, Fertigation Automation, Low maintenance motor controllers,
    Pest prediction, and controlling suggestions, at your fingertips. People moving away from farming due to their modern lifestyle, and this system will let farming easy and make sure food production is consistent.

    AI systems are helping to improve the overall harvest quality and accuracy – known as precision agriculture. AI technology helps in detecting disease in plants, pests and poor nutrition of farms. AI sensors can detect and target weeds and then decide which herbicide to apply within the region.
